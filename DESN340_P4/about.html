About Lotos

Craftsmanship is at the heart of what we do. Each fragrance is a labor of love, a harmonious blend of the finest ingredients sourced from around the world. From the delicate petals of rare flowers to the earthy richness of sustainably harvested woods, we spare no effort in curating a sensory experience that transcends the ordinary.

Founded in 2017, our story is rooted in a passion for both nature and art. We embarked on a mission to redefine the fragrance industry by crafting perfumes that embody sophistication, cruelty-free principles, and a commitment to aesthetics. Our perfumers, each a master of their craft, bring decades of experience and a deep love for the art of scent to every bottle.
