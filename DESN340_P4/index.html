Made with Love

Honestly, this is mostly just because we like the craft of it. There are places that could filter our perfume, package it, send it to a warehouse, ship it out of a fulfilment center. We do these things because keeping close to the product, to the process feels nice. It feels like we are doing something bigger than business.

Made for the Planet

Through 1% for the planet we support the Canadian biosphere by supporting initiatives that are taking charge in regeneration and remediation of ecological damage.

Made for Everybody

Our scents are all gender neutral and meant for any body. Scents don't inherently have a gender. We feel marketing along gendered lines limited exploration. Our greatest hope is you approach our perfume to explore the scent itself and how it makes you feel.
